The Creator – Kieron Brincat

Kieron Brincat is a passionate game programmer with a drive to push the boundaries of player interaction in digital environments. A graduate of the Game Programming degree at South-Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences (XAMK), Kieron’s academic journey culminated in a bold and forward-thinking thesis: AI-Powered Talking NPCs: A New Era of Game Interaction.

Kieron’s interest in artificial intelligence and immersive storytelling began during his early game development projects, where he noticed how often NPC dialogue felt repetitive and disconnected. Motivated by advances in AI and voice recognition technologies, he envisioned a future where in-game characters could respond to players with natural, fluid conversations—just like real people.

With Support From XAMK Gamelab, Kieron Designed A Working Prototype That Integrates:
Real-Time Voice Input Using
Automatic Speech Recognition
(ASR)
AI Dialogue Powered By
Large Language Models
(LLMs)

His research dives deep into the technical architecture, AI model selection, and the player experience, balancing rigorous testing with a creative approach to interaction design. The final product isn’t just a proof of concept—it’s a vision of how storytelling and gameplay could evolve through voice and AI.
